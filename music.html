MY MUSIC

Listen if you want. That's why you're here, is it not?

   Beyond Skyrim: Elsweyr is part of the Beyond Skyrim project, a series of mods for The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim that aims to use existing stories within the game to create entirely new areas for the player to experience. I'm fortunate enough to be a composer for this project alongside professionals, and this project has acted as a driving force to improve my skills and refine my techniques.
   The music I've composed for this project ranges from exploration pieces to combat tracks, and all of it can be found on both Soundcloud and YouTube. Sit down, grab some moon sugar, and stay a while as you lose yourself in the sandy landscape of Anequina.

   Music From Eleatis is a 45 minute collection of tracks written for a close friend of mine's homebrew D&D campaign called "Rise of Chaos". The album contains character themes, battle music, and general themes for the campaign as a whole. From the soft, somber tone of The Seeker of the Past to the heart-pounding energy of The One in Control, this musical work covers a broad range of emotions and styles, and it is by far my longest solo project.

   Turn of the Century is a collection of tracks written for a friend's D&D campaign of the same name. This one is still a work in progress. It starts in Siberia, during the transition between the 1400s and the 1500s, hence the name. Due to its setting, I'm experimenting with many methods of making the very music itself sound cold and unforgiving. From the travel music to the combat music, all of it should sound harsh and wintry. I've only written a handful of tracks for it so far, but as time passes, you'll see more and more pieces added to this playlist on YouTube.
