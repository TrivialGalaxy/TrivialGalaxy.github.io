MY MUSIC

Listen if you want. That's why you're here, is it not?

   Beyond Skyrim: Elsweyr is part of the Beyond Skyrim project, a series of mods for The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim that aims to use existing stories within the game to create entirely new areas for the player to experience. I'm fortunate enough to be a composer for this project alongside professionals, and this project has acted as a driving force to improve my skills and refine my techniques.
   The music I've composed for this project ranges from exploration pieces to combat tracks, and all of it can be found on both Soundcloud and YouTube. Sit down, grab some moon sugar, and stay a while as you lose yourself in the sandy landscape of Anequina.

   Turn of the Century is a collection of tracks written for a friend's D&D campaign of the same name. It starts in Siberia, during the transition between the 1400s and the 1500s, hence the name. Due to its setting, I experimented with many methods of making the very music itself sound cold and unforgiving. From the travel music to the combat music, all of it should sound harsh and wintry. You can listen to the complete collection of tracks with this playlist on YouTube.

   The Oozing is a three volume album written for my own D&D campaign set in the world of Alevar. Each volume corresponds with an act in the main campaign's story, and as the players are now on Act 2, I am working on Vol. 2 at the moment. Due to the setting's fantastical nature as well as the fact that it spans an entire world, I have an opportunity to experiment with different musical styles and textures. I've been making a point to push myself with each track I compose for this album, and to ensure each work is better, or at least different, than the last. Here's the link to a multi-volume playlist on YouTube, and here's a link to Vol. 1 on Spotify.
